Low Light Lounge

Welcome to Low Light Lounge! We are so excited for you to be working here as a bartender! Get ready to step behind the counter to mix, shake, and serve as many drinks as you can during your shift. Your customers are thirsty, and they’re counting on you to craft the perfect cocktail. Think fast, pour with style, and rack up that high score!

How to play

Check the Order – A customer will come up to the bar and request a drink. Pay attention to the ingredients! Pick the Right Ingredients – Click the buttons to add the correct ingredients in the correct order to your shaker. Serve the Drink – When you’ve got all the right ingredients, the drink either has to be stirred or shaked. Your drink will be automatically served. Score Points – If your drink matches the order, you’ll earn points and get a new request. Beat the Clock – You’ve got 60 seconds in each of your shifts to serve as many drinks as possible.

Now, grab your shaker and let’s mix some magic. Good luck, bartender!
